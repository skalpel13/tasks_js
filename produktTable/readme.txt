Product calculator. It is a table in which the user of our site will make their purchases.

Purchases are made using the form. In addition, a link to uninstall is provided for each product. 
The possibility of editing the name, price and quantity of the product has also been made. 
Such editing occurs by double-clicking on a table cell.

Under the table, the total cost of the products is displayed. 
Made it so that this amount was recalculated when deleting and editing products.
